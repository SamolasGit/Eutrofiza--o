O2

Manual

  • Left click: add floating algae.
  • Right click: plant a rooted plant.
  • Moon/Sun button: toggle day and night.
  • Manual button: open/close this panel.
  • Reset button: clear algae, plants, and O2.
  • Day: plants grow if touched by sunlight.
  • Night: plants pause shrink/death from no light.
  • O2 bubbles: bounce in water and expire after 60s.
